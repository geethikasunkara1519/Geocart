Why GeoCart?

Vendies is a revolutionary mobile application designed to bridge the gap between street vendors and consumers. Our app provides a comprehensive platform that allows vendors to set up their e-cart, share live locations, and accept cashless payments. Customers can effortlessly browse local vendors, discover unique products, and make purchases with convenience. With features that enhance both vendor operations and customer experiences, GeoCart not only drives sales but also fosters a sense of community. We are committed to promoting responsible consumption, economic growth, and sustainability while providing street vendors with the tools they need to thrive in a digital world.
